About Double M Advocates

Justice. Integrity. Excellence.

Who We Are

Step into the world of legal excellence with Double M Advocates, a dynamic and forward-thinking law firm nestled in the heart of Thika town. Founded in 2018 by the legal virtuosos Mugo Kamau and Meru Maina, we have quickly emerged as a rising star, offering top-tier legal solutions that rival even the most established names in the industry.

Our journey has been one of remarkable growth. With a loyal and expanding client base, we continue to earn trust through precision, discretion, and unwavering dedication. In just a few years, we have secured retainers from prestigious companies and organizations both within and beyond Thika.

At Double M Advocates, we believe in collaboration. We partner with brilliant, young legal minds, each excelling in their specialties. This enables us to cover a broad spectrum of practice areas and provide comprehensive, modern legal services to our clients.
